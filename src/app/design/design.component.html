This page shows displays the reason why I used the colour scheme I used. The component style, and other information relating to the design.

Why I used purple color:

I used purple colour as the background because it's one of my favourite colours. Purple colour for me represents creativity, wisdom, dignity, grandeur, devotion, peace, pride, mystery, independence, and magic. I also used blue colour because it's a sign of calmness and reliability. The two colours blends perfectly well together

Why I used the flip component:

The flip component also gives the user the ability to view details of the information without further scrolling. It also gives the user a better feel of usability and a better UI design.
